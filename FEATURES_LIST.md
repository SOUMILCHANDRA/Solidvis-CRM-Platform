# 📋 SolidVis CRM: Comprehensive Features List (A-Z)

The SolidVis CRM is a state-of-the-art enterprise command center designed for high-stakes B2B operations. Below is a complete breakdown of every capability enabled in the platform.

---

## 🏛️ Central Command (Dashboard)
- **Real-time Telemetry Counters**: Live estimation of Total Revenue, Total Orders, Registered Companies, and Total Invoices.
- **Holographic Analytics**: Interactive "Revenue Trajectory" line charts with scanline effects and chromatic aberration.
- **Global Intelligence Feed**: A live vertical timeline for sub-second monitoring of invoice lifecycle events (Today/Yesterday grouping).
- **Strategic Intelligence Panel**: Automated alerts for "Invoices Overdue" and identification of "Top Revenue-Contributing Clients."
- **Animated Data Ticking**: High-performance counters that transition smoothly even with millions of records.

---

## 🏢 Corporate Registry (CRM)
- **Advanced Firmographic Data**: Tracking Company Name, GST Number, Cities, and Postal Registry.
- **Universal Search**: Real-time debounced search across 500k+ company records.
- **Geographic Filtering**: Quick-filter registry by City (e.g., Pune, Mumbai).
- **Pagination Engine**: Sub-second navigation for massive result sets.
- **Data Detail View**: Expandable rows (under development) for deep client history.

---

## 📦 Order & Product Management
- **Tile-based Order Grid**: Visual overview of Purchase Orders with Representative and Support status tracking.
- **Product Bifurcation**: A modular order-creation system where multiple products are linked to a single client contract.
- **Real-time Total Projection**: Automatic calculation of projected invoice value during order drafting.
- **Lifecycle Status**: Visual tagging for "Active W/Support," "New," and "Renewal" contracts.

---

## 🧾 Financial Tracker (Invoices & Payments)
- **Status-based Filtering**: Instant isolation of "RECEIVED," "PENDING," or "CANCELLED" liabilities.
- **Amount-Range Search**: Filter financial records by specific INR value ranges (Min/Max).
- **Sub-linear Search**: Debounced search by Invoice ID or Order ID.
- **Data Export Suite**: 
    - 📄 **Professional PDF**: Generates formatted invoice reports with AutoTable.
    - 📊 **CSV Export**: High-speed export of filtered data to Excel/Spreadsheets.
- **Visual Distribution**: Real-time Pie/Dot charts showing Payment Status proportionality.

---

## 🎙️ AI Voice Navigator (Hands-Free)
The CRM mapping utilizes the **Web Speech API** to convert natural language into system state transitions.
- **Navigation Commands**:
    - *"Go to Dashboard"* or *"Show me Home"*
    - *"Show Invoices"* or *"Open Payments"*
    - *"Show Clients"* or *"Top Companies"*
    - *"Open Orders"*
- **Action Commands**:
    - *"Create new order"* or *"Add order"* (Automatically triggers the modal)
- **Intelligent Intent Search**:
    - *"Search for [Term]"*: Automatically navigates to the most relevant tab and fills the search bar.
    - *"Search for [Company Name]"*: Switches to CRM tab and executes the query.
    - *"Search for Unpaid invoices"*: Switches to Finance tab and filters for "PENDING."
    - *"Search for values above 1 lakh"*: Recognizes financial intent and applies amount filters.

---

## 🧠 Strategic AI Assistant
- **Floating AI Orb**: A brand-defining holographic portal (bottom-left) for strategic consultation.
- **Natural Language Analysis**: Ask the AI for high-level summaries (e.g., "Top 5 clients this month," "Overdue risk analysis").
- **Reactive UI**: The AI Orb pulses and glows based on system intelligence events.

---

## 🛡️ Enterprise Core
- **Secure Authentication**: End-to-end encrypted identity management via Supabase GoTrue.
- **Global Search (Ctrl + K)**: A command palette for instant navigation and records lookup.
- **Theme Engine**: Seamless toggle between "Neon Dark High-Contrast" and "Clean Enterprise Light" modes.
- **Aesthetic Foundation**:
    - Glassmorphism UI (Transparency + Blur)
    - Mouse Aura (Glow following the cursor)
    - Floating Particle effects
    - 3D-Tilt interactive cards
